Impact of Soda Tax

Store ID:

Results

In recent years, numerous cities in the U.S. have enacted taxes on beverages to promote health and raise revenue. For example, Berkeley has implemented the soda tax in March, 2015. Using weekly store level sales data from January of 2014 to December of 2015 on stores inside of Berkeley and surrounding areas, we ran a machine learning model to identify the impact of soda tax on store weekly sales using difference in difference approach. In this website, we allow the user to choose the store from the sample: the user can then see the actual tax status of this store and see the plot of actual weekly sales, predicted sales and counterfactual sales if soda tax were implemented for this store.
